Leonardo(Leo)

Height 5'5" (165.1 cm)
Weight 180 lbs (81.72 kg)
Bandana color Blue
Weapon Katana

Leonardo is the leader of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. He is disciplined, focused, and always striving to improve his skills as a fighter and a leader.
Leonardo is known for his proficiency with the katana, a traditional Japanese sword that he wields with grace and precision. He is also a strategist, often coming up with plans and tactics to outsmart his enemies.
Leonardo is highly respected by his brothers, who look up to him for guidance and support. He is a natural leader, with a strong sense of duty and responsibility towards his family and his mission.
