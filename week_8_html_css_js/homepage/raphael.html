Raphael(Raph)

Height 5'5" (165.1 cm)
Weight 197 lbs (89.35 kg)
Bandana color Red
Weapon Ninja Sai

Raphael is the hot-headed member of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. He is often quick to anger and can be stubborn at times, but he also has a fiercely loyal and protective nature towards his brothers.
Raphael is known for his skill with the sai, a traditional Okinawan weapon that he wields with precision and speed. He is also a skilled fighter, capable of taking on multiple opponents at once.
Despite his tough exterior, Raphael has a soft spot for his family and friends, and will do whatever it takes to protect them.
